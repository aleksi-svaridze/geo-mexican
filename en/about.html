Mexican Taco

The Mexican cuisine is distinguished with a variety of cheerful colors and flour. Because of its uniqueness, the UNESCO has been recognized as a part of "World Heritage". Taco is the part of the Mexican cuisine, and it was considered as a "street food" and you could find its variations in any street or corner of Mexico. The origin of Taco is unknown, however, the first wall paintings on the existence of this product were found in 1500 BC in Chiapas. The first Taco Festival was organized in the 18th century by Bernal diaz del kastillo.
